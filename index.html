Ringo Deathstarr

From Austin, Texas, with fuzz

In between Beatles classics and Star Wars paraphernalia lies a hazy purple realm where fuzzy sound waves drown all thought.

Now a shoegaze powerhouse for thousands of fans across the globe; Ringo Deathstarr started their soundblasting career around the year 2007 with series of EP's, but it wasnt until the year 2011 when they started to make ripples across the glove with their Colour Trip full lenght album.

Now, almost 10 years from their debut, their prolofic career has birthed a total of 5 full lenght albums, 6 Ep's and one live recording of their critically aclaimed, most recent eponimous album Ringo Deathstarr.
